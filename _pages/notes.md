	* [Arithmetic level-raising in the even rank case](/files/LevelRaising.pdf), for the London number theory study group on [Liu--Tian--Xiao--Zhang--Zhu](https://arxiv.org/abs/1912.11942).
	* [One Motives](/files/OneMotives.pdf), for the London number theory study group on motives.
